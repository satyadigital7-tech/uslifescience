Privacy Policy

Home Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

US Lifescience ("we", "us", or "our") values the privacy of our visitors, medical professionals, hospital representatives, and distribution partners. This Privacy Policy details the types of information we collect through our corporate website and how we manage, protect, and use that information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you submit directly to us via our online contact and product enquiry forms, including:

  • Personal Details: Name, email address, phone number, and physical office address.
  • Professional Details: Associated hospital, clinic name, pharmacy branch, or distribution company.
  • Form Metadata: Submission IP address, date, time, and subject context.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process collected data exclusively to satisfy standard commercial operations, specifically to:

  • Respond to inquiries, distribute medicine catalogs, and process trade quotes.
  • Provide customer support to clinics, hospitals, and diagnostic institutions.
  • Verify professional identities to ensure compliance with drug sale regulations.

4. Data Protection & Safety

We incorporate industry-standard firewall controls and server validation metrics to secure form communications. All information processed through PHPMailer is handled over encrypted TLS/SSL channels. We do not sell, rent, or lease enquiry lists to third-party marketing companies.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We may use basic session cookies to manage contact form capture fields, validation sessions, and security math captcha values. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though doing so might affect security token checks when submitting enquiries.

6. Changes to this Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated modification date.
